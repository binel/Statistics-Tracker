What is ISU Statitics Tracker?

ISU Statistics Tracker is a web aplication designed to dynamically show the breakdown of enrollment information based college, major, gender,race, and origin. The cahrts and maps generated are dynamically created using D3.js and can be interacted with by the user.

The general page will focus on the six colleges of ISU excluding VET MED as it is a graduate only program. General will show the number of undergraduate students, gender split, nd ethnic deversity per college. There is also a US map show the nuber of students attending ISU per state.

Each college will have the same breakdown of information only focussing on their particular majors. For ese of use and to better represent the data, the charts can be easily sorted with the given options and additional data accoiated with each data piont can be viewed by hovering over the point of interest.


Navigation

To navigate the application, there is a navbar splitting up the sight by general information (which focusses on ISU as a whole), each collge, and by international attendance. Clicking on the college name will take you to that colleges page showing the information for their majors. Each page also has a side bar that will take you to that particular chart on the page.
